Our First Date

My darling Menahil,

I was so nervous today. I must have changed my outfit five times before finally settling on something I hoped you'd like. The butterflies in my stomach were doing somersaults as I waited for you.

When you arrived, all my nervousness melted away. You looked absolutely breathtaking. That little black dress, your hair perfectly framing your face, and that smile - oh that smile could light up cities.

We talked for hours, losing track of time completely. I learned that you're even more wonderful than I imagined. That night, as I walked you home, I realized I never wanted our conversation to end. I think that's when I started falling in love with you.
