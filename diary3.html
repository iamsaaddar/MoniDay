Diary Entry #3

When I Knew I Loved You

My beloved Menahil,

It wasn't any grand gesture or dramatic moment that made me realize I loved you. It was a thousand little things that added up to something I couldn't ignore any longer.

The way your nose crinkles when you laugh. How you always know exactly what to say when I'm feeling down. The warmth in your voice when you say my name. The way you care so deeply about everyone around you.

Today, as we sat in comfortable silence watching the sunset, I knew with absolute certainty that I love you. Not just the idea of you, but every imperfect, beautiful, wonderful part of you. And I think I'll love you for the rest of my life.

Forever Yours
